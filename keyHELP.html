Room Key Help


Need help on how to use/get hold of your room key? Please read the documentation below:
  1. First, download the DoubleTree By Hilton RoomKey app from your app store.*

  2. Sign in with your DoubleTree account by clicking the hamburger menu > Log In

  3. Once you've logged in, your digital room key will appear on the main app page, with the hotel name, location, and your room number

  4. To get into your room, open the DoubleTree By Hilton RoomKey app, and scan your smartphone against the reader**

*Compatible with smartphones running Android 6 or above, iOS 10 or above
**Scanning function only available on smartphones with NFC
