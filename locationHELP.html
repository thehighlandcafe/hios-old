Location Help


Need help on finding our DoubleTree by Hilton with The Highland Cafe(tm) branches? Follow the following steps:
  1. First, click the Back button below or above

  2. Then, once back at the Help page, click the Home button, at the bottom or the top.

  3. Once back on the home screen, click the Our Locations button.

  4. Swipe up and down the page to scroll and view our locations*

*Please note that please DO NOT click directions as you will be redirected off the webapp to Google Maps.
